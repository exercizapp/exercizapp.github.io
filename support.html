About Exerciz

Exerciz is a custom workout-routine builder for iOS. Create routines targeting the muscle groups you want using bodyweight, free weights, or equipment; schedule them through the week; and run guided, timed sessions with an on-screen instructor. You can also browse and save community routines, generate routines with AI, and track your history and stats.

Frequently asked questions

How do I sign in?

Exerciz uses a one-time verification code (OTP). Choose Phone or Email on the sign-in screen, enter your number or address, and type the code we send you. There's no password to remember.

I didn't receive my verification code.

Wait a moment and tap Resend. Check that your phone number or email address is entered correctly, and look in your spam/junk folder for email codes. If it still doesn't arrive, email support@exerciz.app.

How do I create a routine?

On the Workouts tab, tap Add (or New routine) and add exercises from the library. Set the sets, duration, and rest for each, optionally add a warm-up and cool-down, then save. You can schedule it for specific days and times.

How do I start a guided workout?

Open a routine and tap Start Workout. Exerciz guides you through each exercise with a timer and an instructor figure, and moves you through sets, rests, and cool-downs automatically.

Can I add my own exercises?

Yes. You can record a custom exercise with your device camera and use it in your routines. Custom exercise videos stay on your device unless you explicitly choose to share a routine.

How do I change my instructor or profile?

Go to the Profile tab to update your name, age range, fitness level, preferred instructor, units, and AI preferences.

How do I delete my account?

Open the Profile tab, scroll to the bottom, and tap Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data — profile, routines, workout history, and custom exercises. For security you may be asked to verify a one-time code first. This action cannot be undone.

Is my data private?

Your routines, profile, and history are private to your account and are only shared with the community when you explicitly choose to share a routine. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

The app isn't working as expected.

Try closing and reopening the app, and make sure you're on the latest version from the App Store. If the problem continues, email support@exerciz.app with your device model, iOS version, and a short description (a screenshot helps).
